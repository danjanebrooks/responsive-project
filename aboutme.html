About me

My name is Danielle, I live in Manchester and I love Greek food!

My love for Greek food started as a child. As a family we would always go to Greece on holidays and I have lovely fond memories. Greece is a beautiful country and you can nearly always guarantee hot weather. I have always found Greek people very friendly and they are always willing to chat to you and have a good time. Whether it's mainland or one of the small islands, the country is steeped in history and has gorgeous views everywhere you go. It's also where the first theatre plays were performed in the Theatre of Dionysus which was built in Athens and having studied Drama & Theatre at University level, I think that adds to my love for Greece.
I remember when I was younger and on holiday in Greece and trying a Greek salad for the first time. I was unsure on the olives but loved the feta cheese, red onion and the balsamic and oregano dressing. I started to try more and more Greek food from the menus and realised there was nothing I didn't like. From houmous and babaghanoush, to moussaka and Gyros. The food can be indulgent and rich like beef Stifado, or fresh and light like grilled meats and tzatziki. One of my favourite things is that you can eat Greek food in a tapas style way; grazing on lots of small plates of food meaning you can eat more of a variety of different things in one meal!
